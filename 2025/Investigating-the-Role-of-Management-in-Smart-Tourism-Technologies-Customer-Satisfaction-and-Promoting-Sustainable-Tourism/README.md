# Investigating the Role of Management in Smart Tourism Technologies, Customer Satisfaction and Promoting Sustainable Tourism

## Overview

This publication investigates how smart tourism technologies influence tourist satisfaction and sustainable tourism development through effective tourism management.

The study proposes a conceptual framework in which five key smart technology attributes—accessibility, informativeness, interactivity, personalization, and security—directly influence tourist satisfaction, while the tourist experience acts as a mediating factor.

The research highlights the strategic role of tourism management in integrating emerging digital technologies to improve visitor experiences and support sustainable tourism.
