## 🎯 Research Problem

Early-warning systems for identifying at-risk students often rely on centralized machine learning models that require sharing sensitive educational data. This raises important concerns regarding privacy, institutional data governance, and model transparency.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

This study proposes a privacy-preserving federated learning framework that enables multiple educational institutions to collaboratively train student at-risk prediction models without exchanging raw student data.

The framework combines:

* Federated Learning (FedAvg)
* Differential Privacy (DP-SGD)
* Proof-Before-Train (PBT)
* Interpretable Logistic Regression
